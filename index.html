How it works. PDF files are processed in this browser using PDF.js. When OCR is enabled, PDF pages are rendered to an invisible canvas and recognized with Tesseract.js; image files are sent directly to Tesseract.js. Your chosen files are not uploaded to a server by this page, but the OCR engine and language data are downloaded from public CDNs the first time they are needed. OCR is slower than text-layer extraction and works best on clear, high-contrast scans.
